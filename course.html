About Us

Edford University, established by Dr. Francis churchil, stand out as a premier centre of higher learning with a mission of pursuing excellence in education and research.
With their diverse and dynamic community of students, Edford offers a distinctive combination of some of the finest undergraduate, postgraduate and research programs,
accomplished faculty, world-class facilities and a residential campus set on a sprawling 230 acres of sylvan surroundings.
